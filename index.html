The Qur'an is the guiding star for mankind. The man should not only recite the Qur'an,
but must also understand the Qur'an and implement the Qur'an.

We aim to provide you with a beautiful reading experience and a comprehensible understanding experience of the Qur'an.

We aim to do that with support for multi-lingual translations of the Qur'anic verses, so that understanding the Qur'an is as much native to you as you desire.

We aim to do that with accompanied hadith and commentaries on Qur'anic verses which provides greater clarity to the context of the revelations.

We aim to do that by utilizing the latest technologies of the internet in the desktop and mobile space - so that being "on the go" is never an excuse.

We also aim to make it easy for you to develop your own Qur'an apps (if you wish) for the web, mobile and desktop by providing you with easy RESTful APIs.

We aim to provide all of that to you - for free. Yup! It's free.

FAQ

How is it Free?
It is free to use our products. We consider this a lasting-charity for the mankind. May Allah accept all our charities and good deeds with the best of our intentions for serving none but Allah.
Why are you asking for funding if this is meant to be your charity?
The engineers and marketers behind the scene have to work tremendously hard for developing products like these and getting them to your hands. There are server costs, there are software license fees, and there is also a social cost which we have to bear for finding hours beyond our regular paid jobs to support our families. Every penny you spend for this cause is a contribution from you to keep this project running for the sake of Allah - it is a sadaqa (charity) from you too.
